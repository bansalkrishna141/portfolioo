Overview

"FarmBot" is an innovative smart farming assistant designed to revolutionize traditional farming practices. This multifunctional robot assists farmers by detecting plant diseases, sending real-time data, enabling precision spraying of medicine, and automating sowing and harvesting processes.

Additionally, it acts as a scarecrow to protect crops from birds and other pests.

Features

  • Disease Detection: Utilizes advanced sensors and machine learning algorithms to detect plant diseases early and accurately.
  • Real-Time Data Transmission: Sends real-time data to farmers' mobile devices, allowing them to monitor crop health remotely.
  • Precision Spraying: Equipped with a targeted spraying system that allows farmers to apply medicine directly to affected plants, minimizing waste and environmental impact.
  • Automated Sowing and Harvesting: Automates the process of sowing seeds and harvesting crops, reducing labor costs and increasing efficiency.
  • Scarecrow Functionality: Acts as a scarecrow to deter birds and other pests, protecting the crops without the need for manual intervention.

Technologies Used

  • Sensors: High-precision sensors for detecting plant health and environmental conditions.
  • Machine Learning: Algorithms to analyze sensor data and accurately diagnose plant diseases.
  • Robotics: Automated mechanisms for sowing, spraying, and harvesting crops.
  • Wireless Communication: Real-time data transmission to farmers' smartphones or computers via Wi-Fi or Bluetooth.
  • Solar Power: Solar panels to ensure the FarmBot operates sustainably and autonomously.
